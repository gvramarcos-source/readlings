👋 How to Play

A read-together game for a grown-up and a child.

  1. 📖
    A word pops up on the screen.
  2. 🗣️
    Your child reads it out loud.
  3. 👂
    You listen, then tap one:
    ✅ Got it!  they read it right
    🔄 Try again  not yet — no penalty, ever!
  4. 🔊
    Tap the speaker anytime to hear the word.

Get 10 words right and your Readling evolves! 🎉
